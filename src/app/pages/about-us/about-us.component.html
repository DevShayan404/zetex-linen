WHO WE ARE

Weaving Tradition and Innovation for 35+ Years

Welcome to Zetex Linen, a renowned textile manufacturer with a legacy of excellence and a commitment to quality. For over 35 years, we have been weaving tradition and innovation into every thread, crafting exceptional fabrics and textiles that touch lives. From soft furnishings to apparel, our products embody comfort, durability, and style.

With a passion for textile craftsmanship and a dedication to customer satisfaction, we continue to spin the fabric of excellence, one thread at a time. Today, we are a trusted partner for top brands and designers worldwide, delivering exceptional fabrics and textiles that exceed expectations.

OUR MISSION & VALUES

Driven by Excellence & Integrity

At Zetex Linen, our mission is to continuously expand and innovate in the textile industry while maintaining our commitment to excellence and ethical business practices. We strive to provide premium quality products to our global customers while promoting sustainability and responsible manufacturing.

Our core values revolve around customer satisfaction, product quality, environmental responsibility, and creating a lasting impact in the textile market.

  • Excellence: We pursue excellence in everything we do, ensuring top-tier products and services.
  • Sustainability: Committed to reducing our environmental footprint through sustainable practices.
  • Innovation: Constantly innovating to meet the changing demands of the textile industry.
  • Integrity: Operating with transparency and fairness in all our business dealings.
